  • Ease Of Use

    Upload your images instantly using the web interface, windows software, or chrome extension. Once uploaded, a generated link for your image will automatically be copied to your clipboard for you to share right away!

  • Privacy

    So what makes this image hosting service different? While the majority of other services will have public galleries of their user's images, Foto Rift will never expose any photos for any reasons. The only people who will see your photos are the ones that you share the link with.

  • Open Source

    What makes this service even better? Everything is completely open source! Check out my bitbucket page for the entire source code of the website, windows software, and chrome extension.
